DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL INTELLIGENCE

國家情報總監

WASHINGTON, DC

華盛頓，DC

Chairman Graham,

In response to your request for Intelligence Community(IC) information related to the Federal Bureau of Investigation’s (FBI) Crossfire Hurricane Investigation, I have declassified the following:

格雷厄姆主席，

作為對您提交的，關於情報體系（IC）中，聯邦調查局（FBI）《交火颶風調查》相關信息請求的回應，我已將以下信息解密：

- In late July 2016, U.S. intelligence agencies obtained insight into Russian intelligence analysis alleging that U.S. Presidential candidate Hillary Clinton had approved a campaign plan to stir up a scandal against U.S. Presidential candidate Donald Trump by tying him to Putin and the Russians’ hacking of the Democratic National Committee. The IC does not know the accuracy of this allegation or the extent to which the Russian intelligence analysis may reflect exaggeration or fabrication.
- 2016年7月底，美國情報機構從俄羅斯情報分析中獲悉，指控美國總統候選人希拉里-克林頓(Hillary Clinton)批准了一項競選計劃，通過把美國總統候選人唐納德-川普(Donald Trump)和普金(Putin)以及俄羅斯黑客攻擊民主黨全國委員會(DNC)事件聯繫在一起，來引發川普的競選丑聞。美國情報體係無法確認這一指控的準確性，也無法確認俄方的情報分析中可能存在的誇大或捏造的程度。
- According to this handwritten notes, former Central Intelligence Agency Director Brennan subsequently briefed President Obama and other senior national security officials on the intelligence, including the “alleged approval by Hillary Clinton on July 26, 2016 of a proposal from one of her foreign policy advisors to vilify Donal Trump by stirring up a scandal claiming interference by Russian security services.”
- 根據這份手寫的筆記，前中央情報局局長佈倫南(Brennan)隨後向奧巴馬總統(President Obama)和其他高級國家安全官員通報了這份俄方的情報分析，通報內容中包括一下信息：“據稱希拉里-克林頓在2016年7月批准了一位她的外交政策顧問的建議，即通過挑起俄羅斯安全部門干預競選的醜聞來達到中傷唐納德-川普的目的”。
- On 07 September 2016, U.S. intelligence officials forwarded an investigative referral to FBI Director James Comey and Deputy Assistant Director of Counterintelligence Peter Strzok regarding “U.S. Presidential candidate Hillary Clinton’s approval of a plan concerning U.S. Presidential candidate Donald Trump and Russian hackers hampering U.S. elections as a means of distracting the public from use of a private mail server.”
- 2016年9月7日，美國情報官員向聯邦調查局(FBI)局長詹姆斯-科米(James Comey)和反間諜局副助理局長彼得-斯特佐克(Peter Strzok) 轉發了一份關於“美國總統候選人希拉克-克林頓(Hillary Clinton)批准一項利用美國總統候選人唐納德-川普(Donald Trump)和俄羅斯黑客阻礙美國大選為藉口，轉移公眾對其違規使用私人郵件服務器的注意力的計劃”的調查報告。


As referenced in his 24 September 2020 letter to your Committee, Attorney General Barr has advised that the disclosure of this information will not interfere with ongoing Department of Justice investigations. Additional declassification and public disclosure of related intelligence remains under consideration; however, the IC welcomes the opportunity to provide a classified briefing with further detail at your convenience.

恭敬的
