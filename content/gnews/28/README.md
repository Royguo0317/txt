**我们要改变西方人眼里的中国人刻板形象**

**We Ought to Change the Stereotyped Image of Chinese in the Eyes of the West**

我觉得中国人在世界上的那种刻板的形象，很让我不舒服，无论走到世界各地，我跟欧洲人在一起，我说你们怎么看待中国人？所有的人都是..只要说实话的人都说中国人刻板，都是烧饼脸，而且是不拘的那种表情，不苟言笑，拘谨过度，苦大仇深，而且那种不自信得慌的眼神，紧张兮兮、木纳、呆板。真的是外国人最了解中国人的、最正派就是李小龙，李小龙对中国人的影响很大。这样很不舒服的，我觉得中国人不是这样子的。

Another reason is that I feel that the stereotyped image of the Chinese in the world makes me feel really uncomfortable, no matter where I go in the world. When I am with European people and ask them, “What do you think of the Chinese?” All people say, as long as they are truthful, that the Chinese are stereotyped, having flat faces, unsmiling, overly cautious, like having been through much bitterness, and with the kind of unconfident eyes, looking nervous, insensitive, and stiff. The Chinese whom foreigners know the best and have the most positive impression about is Bruce Lee. Bruce Lee had great influence on Chinese’ image. So it is very uncomfortable [to hear those negative comments about Chinese]. I don’t think real Chinese are like that.

所以说我觉得在爆料革命当中，改变中国人的形象，这是一个非常非常重要的事情。我不希望中国女人的那种勤劳、善良和睿智和中国女人比男人勇敢的那一面，还有中国男人，真的幽默，记忆力非常好，勤劳，而且中国男人这种意志很坚强，小钢炮，非常非常耐用，大家不要想多了啊，非常非常耐用。

So I think it is a very, very important thing to change the image of the Chinese via the Whistleblower Movement. I don’t want the hard-working, kind and wise Chinese women, who are braver than men, and the Chinese men, who are really humorous, with very good memory, hardworking, steadfast, like small steel cannons, very very durable (don’t think too much, okay), to go unknown.

我觉得这些优良的品质，应该要西方知道。我们不是西方的媒体、好莱坞，还有共产党给世界带来的那样。那不能代表中国人。好莱坞过去的电影和文化作品，还有艺术作品，还有娱乐作品，所有刻画出来的中国人，都是不正确的，中国人被共产党代表了那么多年，在西方所被代表的中国人的形象，也不是那样子的。所以说我觉得我们新中国联邦、爆料革命要改变中国人的形象，就这么简单。

I think these good qualities should be known to the West. We are not like what the Western media, Hollywood, and the CCP let the world believe. They can’t represent the Chinese. The Chinese people portrayed by Hollywood’s old movies and other cultural, art, and entertainment works are not real. The Chinese people have been represented by the CCP for so many years. And the image made by the Chinese in the West is not true. That’s why I think our New Federal State of China and Whistleblower Movement need to change the image of Chinese people. Simple as it is.
